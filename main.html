The Shawshank Redemption

1994 R 2h 22m
IMDb Rating: 9.3/10

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency. Andy Dufresne, a banker convicted of murdering his wife and her lover despite his claims of innocence, finds himself serving a life sentence at Shawshank State Penitentiary. There he befriends Ellis "Red" Redding, a contraband smuggler, and becomes instrumental in a money-laundering operation led by the prison warden. Over the years, Andy retains hope and eventually engineers an elaborate escape.
